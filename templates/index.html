How Safe is San Francisco?

Visualizing Criminal Activity in the City by the Bay

In an effort to better understand the changing patterns of crime in San Francisco over the years, we invite our users to explore the provided graphics. Select a year to see breakdown of the occurence of crime in each one. Scroll down to view a map of every recorded incident since 2018 by police district. Note that data has been cleaned of offense categories less relevant to the health and safety of the average resident or visitor. These eliminated categories of offense include but are not limited to non-criminal, mischief, forgery, loitering, and warrants.
