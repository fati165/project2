How Safe is San Francisco?

Visualizing Criminal Activity in the City by the Bay

In an effort to better understand the changing patterns of crime in San Francisco over the years, we invite our users to explore the provided graphics. To the left we have a chart and below we have provided a map as well as a drop down menu of target years. When one of the three available years is selected, the graph and map will change to reflect the data collected for that year.

data for this project was sourced from the SFPD's annual crime report
